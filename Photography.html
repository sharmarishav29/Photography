About Us

I'm Gurpreet Singh, the lens enthusiast behind the camera at Get It Clicked. With a deep-rooted passion for storytelling through images, I embark on a visual journey to capture the beauty of life's fleeting moments. My photographic style is a blend of artistry and authenticity, aiming to freeze not just smiles and poses but the genuine emotions that make each moment unique. As a dedicated photographer, I specialize in Portrait Photography and organizing events. What sets my work apart is the commitment to creating a comfortable and enjoyable experience for every client. I believe that the best photos arise when the atmosphere is relaxed, allowing personalities to shine through. Having honed my craft over 10 years, I've had the privilege of documenting diverse stories and connecting with wonderful individuals.
